Ne kemi ushqime te ndryshme, por me te shendetshmet jane pemet dhe perimet, pra ato qe vijne te gatshme nga natyra, kurse ushqimet te cilat jane me te demshme per organizmin tone jane: ushqimet e shpejta, embelsirat, etj.Per te si nje organizm te shendoshe duhet qe te hajme sa me shume peme dhe perime dhe sa me pak ushqime te shpejta dhe embelsira.Nese ne pakesojme perdorimin e pemeve dhe perimeve dhe hame me shume ushqime te shpejta dhe embelsira tek ne mund te shfaqen semundje te ndryshme te cilat mund te na lene pasoja fatale..

Welcome to restaurant

Food is one of the most important factors for human life. Foods help the game of organisms. They also together with water and air conspire for living conditions. We have different foods, but the healthiest are fruits and vegetables, so those that come ready from nature, while the foods that are most harmful to our body are: fast food, sweets, etc. For him as an organism to be healthy we should eat as many fruits and vegetables and as little fast food and sweets. If we reduce the use of fruits and vegetables and eat more fast foods and sweets we can develop various diseases which can to leave us fatal consequences .
